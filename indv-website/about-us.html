About “Utah Resorts”

About Utah Resorts, our goal of this website is to provide you, the rider with easy access and a free format for information on your local Utah resorts, and the ability to comment and provide info about the ski resorts of Utah. Our hope is to help the guests of these local resorts find the right place for them and their family/friends, so that you can have the best experience on the mountain. In addition to help you find the right place. We would like to give the opportunity for the people who have visited these resorts, to give feedback. And maybe help others find the right place for them. The way this will be broken up is into three main sections of information

1. Basic information

2. Reviews/comments

3. Map and visuals of resort

Each Resort will be clearly laid out on the main page with the accompanying logo of said Ski Resort, with easy access to browse through and see which one you are looking for. By doing this you will be able seek out your Resort, and find the work hours, pricing, and tips for this resort. Under each category we will have the information formatted as follows



































1. Basic information

Under this category we will have, the schedule and days that the Resort will be opened. As well you will have the number of trails opened and the map of each resorts trail. A provided address, and pricing information with a number to contact the front desk.



































2. Reviews/comments

This is really what sets us apart from other websites. In this section you will receive personal reviews and personal thoughts on the resorts. This way you can get an un biased idea of the resort itself in knowing the what it is you are lily to get when visiting. In addition to these kinda reviews, there will also be a comment section, this will allow a larger variety when it comes to the opinions of the resort itself. Some of the things that will be reviewed and commented on is 1. groomed trails and paths 2.terrain parks, high jumps, half pipes and other services of this nature, 3. off trail sites, like tress runs. And steep mountain faces and hikes



































3. Map and visuals of resort.

Finally, you will receive some visuals of the resorts, and the trails and where they fall in play. Helping see for yourself what some of the attractions look like, this allows you to see for yourself and know what you will be visiting. the whole purpose is to help locals and visitors get a better un biased view on the Ski resorts and know what it is they will be getting into.
